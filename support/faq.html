Support

Frequently asked questions.

Grouped by topic. Can't find your answer? Contact us directly.

Company

Is SEE IT, OWN IT. a real company?

Yes. SEE IT, OWN IT. LTD is a private company limited by shares, registered in England & Wales under company number 11765653. Full registration details are listed on our About page.

Where are you based?

Our registered office is 37 South Park Road, Liverpool, L32 2AG, United Kingdom.

How many apps do you currently publish?

Our first app is currently in development. We intentionally don't list apps before they're real and live — check the Apps page for current status.

Privacy & data

What data do your apps collect?

It depends on the specific app — each app has its own delta section within our Apps Privacy Policy describing exactly what it accesses and why. We follow a minimal-permission approach across the board.

How do I request that my data be deleted?

Visit our Data Deletion page for the current process. In short: email privacy@seeitownit.site with your request and we'll confirm once it's actioned.

Do you sell user data to third parties?

No. We do not sell personal data. Full detail is in the Apps Privacy Policy.

Do you use cookies or analytics on this website?

See our Cookie Policy for the current and up-to-date list of any cookies or analytics tools in use.

Using our apps

An app isn't working as expected — what should I do?

Email support@seeitownit.site with the app name, your device model, and a description of the issue. The more detail you give us, the faster we can help.

Are your apps free?

Pricing varies per app and is shown on each app's Google Play listing. We'll never charge for something without making the cost clear before you install.

Will apps keep receiving updates?

Maintaining what we ship is a core value — see our About page. We don't publish and abandon.

Security

How do I report a security vulnerability?

Email security@seeitownit.site with details. We take good-faith disclosure seriously and will respond as a priority.
