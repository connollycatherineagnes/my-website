What cookies are

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site function, remember preferences, or understand how it's used. This policy covers cookies and similar technologies (such as local storage) used on seeitownit.site.

Cookies we use

Type Purpose Consent required?
Essential (theme preference) Remembers your light/dark mode choice via browser local storage. No — strictly necessary for site function
Analytics Not currently active. This table will be updated the moment any analytics tool is enabled. Yes, when enabled

Managing cookies

Most browsers let you block or delete cookies via their settings. Blocking essential cookies (like the theme preference) simply means the site falls back to your system's default light/dark setting each visit — it won't break core functionality.

Third-party cookies

Google Fonts is used to load our typefaces, which may involve a request to Google's servers. We do not currently use third-party advertising or tracking cookies. If this changes, this policy will be updated in advance of the change taking effect.
