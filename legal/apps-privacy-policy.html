Scope of this policy

This is the master Privacy Policy for every Android application published by SEE IT, OWN IT. LTD (company number 11765653) under the developer name SEE IT, OWN IT. It covers data practices common to all our apps. Where a specific app collects something beyond what's described here, that app will have its own named subsection under Per-app data notes below, and its Google Play "Data Safety" listing will match this document.

Data we may collect

Depending on the specific app, we may collect:

  • Account data — if an app offers accounts, this may include an email address and a password (stored using industry-standard hashing, never in plain text).
  • App usage data — feature usage and crash/error logs, used solely to fix bugs and improve the app.
  • Device information — device model, OS version, and app version, used for compatibility and debugging.
  • Content you create within an app — such as saved items, notes, or preferences, where relevant to that app's function.

We follow a minimal-permission principle: an app will only ever request the Android permissions it genuinely needs to function.

Data we do not do

  • We do not sell personal data to third parties.
  • We do not collect data beyond what a specific app's declared purpose requires.
  • We do not use dark patterns to extract consent for data collection.

Legal basis for processing

Where UK GDPR applies, we rely on: performance of a contract (providing the app's core functionality), legitimate interest (crash reporting, basic diagnostics), and consent (for anything optional, such as analytics or marketing communications, where offered).

Data retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purpose it was collected for, or as required by law. Account data is retained until you delete your account or request deletion via our Data Deletion process. Crash and diagnostic logs are typically retained for a limited rolling window before automatic deletion.

Third-party services

Specific third-party processors (e.g. crash reporting, analytics, or cloud hosting providers) used by a given app will be listed in that app's per-app data notes below, once finalised. Where a third party processes data on our behalf, we ensure a data processing agreement is in place consistent with UK GDPR requirements.

International data transfers

Server/hosting location to be confirmed and detailed here. Where personal data is transferred outside the UK or EEA, we ensure an appropriate safeguard applies, such as adequacy regulations or standard contractual clauses.

Children's privacy

Unless an app is explicitly marketed as being designed for children, our apps are not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children under that age. If an app is designed with a younger audience in mind, its per-app data notes will state the additional safeguards in place, consistent with Google Play Families Policy requirements.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict, or port your personal data, and to object to certain processing. To exercise these rights, use our Data Deletion page or email privacy@seeitownit.site. You may also lodge a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ico.org.uk).

Per-app data notes

No apps are currently live. When our first app launches, its specific data practices — permissions used, any third-party SDKs, and any data types beyond this master policy — will be documented here as a named subsection, and linked directly from that app's page and its Google Play Data Safety listing.

Security

We apply reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect personal data against unauthorised access, alteration, or loss, including encryption in transit and minimal-permission app design. No system is perfectly secure; if you believe you've found a vulnerability, please report it via security@seeitownit.site.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as our apps evolve. Material changes will be reflected in the effective date above, and — where a change affects data already collected — we will take reasonable steps to notify active users within the relevant app.

Contact

Privacy questions or data requests: privacy@seeitownit.site. This policy is governed by the laws of England and Wales.

Questions about this document?

Email us at privacy@seeitownit.site. We aim to respond to all legal and privacy enquiries promptly.
