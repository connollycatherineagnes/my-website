About us

A small studio with a specific point of view.

We think software should say what it does, do what it says, and stay out of your way otherwise. Here's who we are and why we build this way.

Our story

SEE IT, OWN IT. LTD was registered in Liverpool as a UK private limited company. What started as a single-app idea has grown into a studio building a small, deliberate portfolio of Android applications — each one designed around clarity, respect for user data, and long-term maintenance rather than a one-off release and silence.

We're still early. The catalogue is small today. But the infrastructure behind it — the legal framework, the support channels, the privacy commitments — is built for a studio with many apps, not just one, because that's the plan.

Mission & vision

Mission — to design Android applications that are simple to understand, transparent about the data they use, and genuinely useful in daily life, without the bloat or over-collection common in mass-market apps.

Vision — to become a recognised UK independent software studio known for a growing portfolio of trustworthy applications, expanding over time into a multi-platform product company.

What we stand for

Core values

Transparency

Plain-language privacy practices. No hidden data collection, ever.

Craft over volume

Fewer, better apps — not a large low-quality catalogue.

User ownership

You control your data and your experience. No forced subscriptions.

Accountability

A real company, real contact channels, real legal grounding behind every app.

Continuous improvement

Apps are maintained for the long run, not abandoned after release.

Security-minded

Minimal permissions and a real disclosure channel for researchers.
